Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how panoskokmotos.com ("this site", "I", "me") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this website.

1. Information Collected

This site collects limited, anonymised data through third-party analytics tools:

  • Google Analytics — collects anonymised usage data (pages visited, time on site, device/browser type, approximate geographic region). No personally identifiable information is collected. Data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • PostHog — collects anonymised analytics to understand how visitors interact with this site. Data is subject to PostHog's Privacy Policy.

Analytics cookies are only loaded after you give explicit consent via the cookie banner.

2. Contact Form

If you submit a message through the contact form, the information you provide (name, email, message) is sent directly to me and used solely to respond to your enquiry. This data is not stored in any database or shared with third parties.

3. Cookies

This site uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — used to remember your cookie consent preference. These are always active.
  • Analytics cookies — used by Google Analytics and PostHog to collect anonymised usage statistics. These are only set if you choose to accept.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site.

4. Third-Party Services

This site embeds or links to the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • TidyCal — calendar booking
  • Google Analytics — analytics
  • PostHog — analytics
  • Plausible Analytics — privacy-first analytics

5. Data Retention

I do not store any personal data on servers I control. Analytics data is retained according to the respective third-party service's policies. Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for analytics cookies at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out at panagiotis.kokmotoss@gmail.com.
