About Me

Entrepreneur building for social impact

Born in Patras, Greece, I grew up in a low-income family — which taught me to value the small, important things in life and live with purpose over comfort. Watching my family come home tired and unmotivated was a crucial mindshift: I committed to getting educated, understanding how the world works, and taking risks.

I studied Mechanical Engineering at the University of Patras (Top 3%, GPA 3.5), spent a semester at UPC Barcelona on Erasmus, and turned that foundation into a mission: contribute to a better tomorrow.

I co-founded Givelink in 2022 — an in-kind donation platform where donors shop for exactly what nonprofits need. No cash abstraction, just real goods, real impact, real stories.

Beyond Givelink, I host Entrepreneurship Talks, serve as a WEF Global Shaper in San Francisco, and sit on the Executive Board of Social Impact SF. Ultimately, I want to provide value to every young person facing economic difficulties — and help them believe in their future.
