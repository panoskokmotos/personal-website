About Me

Entrepreneur building for social impact

I'm a mechanical engineer turned entrepreneur with a mission to learn and grow continuously — and a vision to contribute toward a better tomorrow.

I co-founded Givelink in 2022 — an in-kind donation platform where donors shop for exactly what nonprofits need. No cash abstraction, just real goods, real impact, real stories.

Beyond Givelink, I host Entrepreneurship Talks, serve as a WEF Global Shaper (San Francisco), and sit on the Executive Board of Social Impact SF.
