About Me

I'm a mechanical engineer turned entrepreneur with a passion for building products that create real social impact. I co-founded Givelink in 2022, an in-kind donation platform connecting donors directly with nonprofits' most needed goods — cutting out the friction of cash donations and delivering measurable, story-driven impact.

Beyond Givelink, I host Entrepreneurship Talks, a podcast with 350,000+ views featuring conversations with inspiring founders and leaders. I'm a Global Shaper at the World Economic Forum (San Francisco Hub) and serve on the Executive Board of Social Impact SF.

I thrive at the intersection of business, technology, and social impact — and I'm always looking for bold people working on hard problems.
